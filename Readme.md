## Encoder/Decoder ASCII85
---
Programm works like a default base64 utility
---
### firstly use make or for quick build:
    - main programm:
        ./build_main.sh
    - tests:
        ./build_tests.sh
---
### then
### for encoding:
    ./ascii85
    ./ascii85 -e
---
### for decoding:
    ./ascii85 -d
---
### for tests:
    ./tests
---
(argument may be -e or -d, or doesnt)
